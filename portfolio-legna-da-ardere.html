LEGNA DA ARDERE

Website for a wood company

I created this website for a friend who owns a small firewood business.
It’s a showcase website designed to present both the company and its products.

The site is divided into 5 sections: the "home" page, where the company is introduced; the "prodotti" page, which showcases the full range of products; the "legna in sacchi" page, a dedicated section for packaged firewood; the "informazioni" page, which helps customers choose the most suitable product based on the type of stove they have at home; and finally, the "contatti" page, where all the company's contact details and social media links can be found.

I built the site according to the instructions provided by the business owner, aiming for a clean and effective design. I also took into account the needs of regular customers by keeping the site easy to use and accessible.

In addition, I tried to stick to a color palette that closely matches the company's logo — green, brown, black, and white — to maintain a coherent visual identity throughout the website.

Technologies: HTML, CSS, JAVASCRIPT, VUE JS, BOOTSTRAP, DIGITAL OCEAN, NGINX
